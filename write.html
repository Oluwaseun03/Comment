Differences between paddings, margins and borders
  • The difference between paddings, margins, and borders
  • Padding is the inner space between the content and the border of your box. It is used to create space around an element content,inside any defined borders
  • Margin is the outer space surrounding the box. It is used to add space between an image and the description of that image
  • Border is a box that goes around the padding and content
